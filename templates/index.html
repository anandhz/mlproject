‣ Gender : Refers to the social and cultural roles, behaviors, attributes, and expectations associated with being male or female.

‣ parental_level_of_education : Parental education can influence a student's educational expectations and choices, such as the likelihood of pursuing higher education.

‣ Writing_score : student's ability to convey thoughts, ideas, and information in a coherent and organized manner.

‣ Performance_score : Overall performance of the students.
